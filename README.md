# AI Strategic Intelligence Dashboard – India 🇮🇳

## 📌 Overview

This project presents a **consulting-style analytics dashboard** analyzing the growth, adoption, and strategic opportunities of Artificial Intelligence (AI) across industries in India.

The dashboard synthesizes insights from multiple industry reports to evaluate:

* Market size and growth projections
* Sector-wise AI maturity and adoption
* Key bottlenecks in scaling AI
* Strategic opportunities for enterprises

---

## 🎯 Objective

To transform fragmented industry data into a **decision-making framework** that helps identify:

* Which sectors are ready to scale AI
* Where investment and capability building are required
* Key barriers to enterprise AI adoption

---

## 📊 Key Insights

* 🇮🇳 India’s AI market is projected to reach **~$17B by 2027**
* 📈 Expected growth of **~25–35% CAGR**
* 🚀 **BFSI and IT & ITeS** show high maturity and high opportunity → ideal for scaling AI
* 📉 **Healthcare and Retail** require investment in data infrastructure and capabilities
* 🏛️ **Government sector** represents long-term transformation potential

---

## 🧠 Dashboard Components

### 1. KPI Summary

* AI Market Size (2027)
* Average AI Maturity Score
* AI Market CAGR

---

### 2. Market Growth Forecast

Visualizes projected AI market growth (2023–2027) under:

* Base scenario
* High growth scenario
* Low growth scenario

---

### 3. Sector Maturity Breakdown

Evaluates industries across:

* Data readiness
* Talent availability
* AI adoption

---

### 4. AI Pilot-to-Production Funnel

Illustrates drop-off from:

* Ideas → Pilot → Validated → Production → Scaled AI

Highlights key bottlenecks in enterprise AI scaling.

---

### 5. Opportunity vs Maturity Matrix (Core Insight)

A strategic 2x2 framework mapping sectors based on:

* AI maturity (X-axis)
* Market opportunity (Y-axis)

#### Strategic Interpretation:

* 🚀 High maturity + high opportunity → Scale AI
* 📈 Low maturity + high opportunity → Invest & build
* ⚙ Medium maturity → Optimize adoption
* ❌ Low priority → Limited immediate focus
